About

I'm Ruqaiyyah, but I go by Kya or Rose. I'm a 22yrs old graphic designer born in Philadelphia, Pa. I describe myself as alternative goth and angelic. I have been an artist for years, and art has been one of my major strengths. Art, to me, is often considered the process or product of deliberately arranging elements in a way that appeals to the senses or emotions. It encompasses various activities, creations, and methods of expression, including drawing, graphic design, music, gaming, acting, sculpture, and paintings. This is why I feel like I'm very skillful.
